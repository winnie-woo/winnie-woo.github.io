Goulash

This is a Hungarian braised beef.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Apple
  • Onion
  • carrots
  • Juniper
  • Black Pepper
  • salt
  • Red Wine

Directions

  1. Cut the beef in to pieces
  2. add olive oil, mixed beef and onion
  3. stir fry the beef and the onion
  4. Add apple ,ashed and carrot mashed
  5. mix all the ingredients
  6. put a cup of red wind, and few juniper
  7. cooking for 1 hour
  8. put some salt and black pepper when you are reay to eat
